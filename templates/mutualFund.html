Reliance Liquid Fund Treasury Plan (IP) – G

Reliance Liquid Fund ensure that your investments are risk free, no-lock in on withdrawl and generate stable returns. This fund primarily invests in money market instruments of public sector banks and undertakings such as HUDCO, L&T and Tata Steel making it ultra-safe (almost as safe as your savings bank deposits) to invest your money.

Time Period* Annualized Returns
1 Month 9.48%

Current AUM: Rs. 14,469 Crores

Tax Benefit: Unlike FD, there is no TDS for investments in this fund. Also, for investments more than 3 years, tax payout becomes negligible as there is indexation benefits. However for investments less than 3 years, you will have to declare the returns from this investment at the time of tax filing and pay tax as per your salary bracket.
